Fees you'll pay

You must pay the claim fee up front. However, we'll add it to the amount you're claiming back from the respondent.
Claim fee £70
Hearing fee (if your claim goes to a hearing) £115
Other fees - for example, to update your claim once submitted varies by case
How these fees are calculated

The court fee is based on the amount you're claiming, including any interest.
