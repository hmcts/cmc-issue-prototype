Money claim rules

It could count against you if you go to a hearing and have not tried to resolve your dispute out of court.

Before you make a claim

You must try to solve the dispute directly before making a claim:

  • Speak to them (send an email or letter if you want a record).
  • Tell them what you believe they owe you and why.
  • Explain that if you can't reach an agreement (eg a timetable for paying the money) you'll make a legal money claim.
  • Tell them they might have to pay the full amount as well as fees and interest if you claim, and that they could get a County Court Judgment (CCJ).

After you've claimed

Try to settle your case before a hearing even after you have submitted your claim to the court.

You can use our service to help you resolve your dispute and reach an agreement, if needed.
