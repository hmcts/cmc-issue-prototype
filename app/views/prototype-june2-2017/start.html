Issue civil court proceedings

Who this service is for

You must be a legal representative to use this service.

What this service does

This service is an alternative to posting a completed N1 claim form - it allows you to:

  • get civil court proceedings issued by the County Court online
  • instantly calculate the court fee
  • download the sealed claim form to serve on the defendant yourself

It normally takes 15 minutes to complete


Start now

Before you start

You'll need:

  • current addresses for all claimants and defendants
  • the address where you want the claim to be served
  • a credit card or Fee Account to pay the issue fee.

You must be a legal representative and have your client's permission to issue the claim.
