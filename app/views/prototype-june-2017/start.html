Issue a money claim

Use this service to:

  • get an unspecified civil money claim issued by the County Court
  • download the sealed claim form to serve on the defendant yourself

You can only use this service if you:

  • want to serve the claim yourself
  • don't need to enter particulars of claim when you issue

You can't use this service to apply for help with fees at the same time as issuing the claim.

Before you start

You'll need:

  • current addresses for all claimants and defendants
  • the address where you want the claim to be served
  • a credit card or Fee Account to pay the issue fee.

You must be a legal representative and have your client's permission to issue the claim.
